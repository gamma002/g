About Us

God's Intentions Fellowship

God's Intentions Fellowship is being overseen by Prophetess Gladys Mwila Zulu (The Lioness), who is assisted by her husband, Prophet Mike Zulu.

How God's Intentions Fellowship Was Born

God's Intentions Fellowship was born by the word of instruction from Prophet Samuel Kakande on 16th October 2023 and was officially birthed on 20th October 2023 at Lusaka Boys Main Wall, Longacres.

The first service was attended by 50 people. Since then, the fellowship has been graciously growing, now reaching over 400 attendees per week. Additionally, God's Intentions Fellowship has online members all over Zambia and international members as well.

Our Growth and Expansion

God's Intentions Fellowship has expanded to other towns in Zambia, including Kabwe, Kitwe, and Livingstone, where prayer meetings are held once every month.
